Hobby

One of my favorite hobbies is making jewllery. I have only been doing it for a few months, but I've already made over 100 peices, from necklaces to bracelets to rings. I started making my own jewllery because I wanted to have things to wear for high school. I wanted to start fresh, and that included updating my wardrobe to better fit my personality. I had been getting into fashion more around that time, and I felt like I had more energy to care about my apperance (well, maybe not that much energy), So I wanted to have things to acessorize with. I borrowed some of my mom's beads to make some bracelets and when she saw I was taking an interest we bought some more beads together.
